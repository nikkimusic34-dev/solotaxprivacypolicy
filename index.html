SoloTax

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

SoloTax does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device.

Overview

SoloTax ("the app") is a tax tracking tool for freelancers and self-employed individuals. This policy explains how we handle your information. The short version: we don't collect any of it.

Data we do not collect

SoloTax does not collect, store on any server, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your income, expenses, or financial data
  • Your location or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data to third-party advertising or analytics services

We have no user accounts. No data ever leaves your device.

Data stored on your device

All data you enter into SoloTax — including income entries, deductions, mileage logs, and settings — is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage (AsyncStorage). This data is:

  • Only accessible by the SoloTax app on your device
  • Included in your device's standard iCloud or iTunes backup if you have those enabled
  • Permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Push notifications

SoloTax can send local notifications to remind you of upcoming quarterly tax deadlines. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server to generate them. You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone's Settings.

We use Expo's notification service solely to schedule local reminders. No notification content or delivery data is collected.

Exports

SoloTax allows you to export your data as a PDF or CSV file using your device's native share sheet. When you export, you choose where the file goes — we have no visibility into or access to exported files.

Children's privacy

SoloTax is intended for adults managing their own tax obligations. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. Because we collect no information at all, this requirement is met by default.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Any meaningful changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
